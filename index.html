About STEIM Academy

The Possible
Uganda

STEIM Academy is Uganda's pioneering STEM-to-income education programme — built on one powerful premise: every student has a product inside them waiting to reach the market.

We don't just teach science and technology. We transform STEM knowledge into real market solutions, real products, and real income — so students graduate not just literate, but economically empowered.

"Uganda's next generation of wealth creators are sitting in classrooms right now — waiting for someone to hand them the tools."

Founded and piloted across Gayaza High School and Kira-Mulawa programs, STEIM Academy has already catalysed 753 students, generated 36+ market-ready products, and created a model proven ready for national scale.

Our Three Programs

The Innovation
Pathways

Three interconnected avenues that take a student from raw idea all the way to market-proven product — and income.

Pathway 01
STEIM Bootcamp
Idea Incubation Avenue

Intensive hands-on training to help students create or refine their ideas into market-demand solutions — turning STEM knowledge into real-world products.

  • Hands-on problem solving sprints
  • Market research & validation
  • Prototype development
  • Pitch preparation & delivery

Pathway 02
STEIM Market Day
Monetisation Avenue

A live marketplace to showcase, support, and grow innovative STEM ideas and products — helping students scale buyers for their creations. Buy. Swap. Invest.

  • Live student product showcase
  • Real buyer & investor access
  • Sales, swaps & investment pitching
  • Media & brand exposure

Pathway 03
STEIM Clinics
Product Perfection Avenue

Classes, workshops, and trainings to help students tweak, fix, and perfect their products for production readiness and market success. From idea to excellence.

  • Product quality workshops
  • Expert mentorship sessions
  • Market-readiness assessment
  • Iterative product refinement

To East Africa

Proof of Concept

Active Phase
2026 · Year 2
20 Schools Across Uganda

Scaling partnerships to 20 schools across Kampala, Wakiso, and Mukono. Launch of formal STEIM Market Day events. First corporate sponsorships and CSR partnerships activated. Curriculum standardised for national replication.

Scale Phase

Regional Innovation Hubs

Physical STEIM Hub facilities in Kampala, Gulu, Mbarara, and Mbale. Training-of-trainers program deployed to build a national facilitator network. Digital platform launched for remote student engagement.
